# Greater Restoration

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target's {@condition exhaustion} level by one, or end one of the following effects on the target: One effect that {@condition charmed} or {@condition petrified} the target One curse, including the target's attunement to a cursed magic item Any reduction to one of the target's ability scores One effect reducing the target's hit point maximum
