# Reflection

*Medium fey, Chaotic Evil*

### Traits
***Amorphous.*** The reflection can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
***Shadow Stealth.*** While in dim light or darkness, the reflection can take the {@action Hide} action as a bonus action.
***Sunlight Weakness.*** While in sunlight, the reflection has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws.

### Actions
***Strength Drain.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 4} to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. {@h}9 ({@damage 2d6 + 2}) necrotic damage, and the target's Strength score is reduced by 1d4. The target dies if this reduces its Strength to 0. Otherwise, the reduction lasts until the target finishes a short or long rest. If a non-evil humanoid dies from this attack, a new reflection rises from the corpse 1d4 hours later.
