# Salamander

*Large elemental, Neutral Evil*

### Traits
***Heated Body.*** A creature that touches the salamander or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 7 ({@damage 2d6}) fire damage.
***Heated Weapons.*** Any metal melee weapon the salamander wields deals an extra 3 ({@damage 1d6}) fire damage on a hit (included in the attack).

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The salamander makes two attacks: one with its spear and one with its tail.
***Spear.*** {@atk mw,rw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) piercing damage, or 13 ({@damage 2d8 + 4}) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack, plus 3 ({@damage 1d6}) fire damage.
***Tail.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) bludgeoning damage plus 7 ({@damage 2d6}) fire damage, and the target is {@condition grappled} (escape {@dc 14}). Until this grapple ends, the target is {@condition restrained}, the salamander can automatically hit the target with its tail, and the salamander can't make tail attacks against other targets.
