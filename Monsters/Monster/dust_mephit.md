# Dust Mephit

*Small elemental, Neutral Evil*

### Traits
***Death Burst.*** When the mephit dies, it explodes in a burst of dust. Each creature within 5 feet of it must then succeed on a {@dc 10} Constitution saving throw or be {@condition blinded} for 1 minute. A {@condition blinded} creature can repeat the saving throw on each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

### Actions
***Claws.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 4} to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. {@h}4 ({@damage 1d4 + 2}) slashing damage.
***Blinding Breath {@recharge}.*** The mephit exhales a 15-foot cone of blinding dust. Each creature in that area must succeed on a {@dc 10} Dexterity saving throw or be {@condition blinded} for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
