# Winter Wolf

*Large monstrosity, Neutral Evil*

### Traits
***Keen Hearing and Smell.*** The wolf has advantage on Wisdom ({@skill Perception}) checks that rely on hearing or smell.
***Pack Tactics.*** The wolf has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't {@condition incapacitated}.
***Snow Camouflage.*** The wolf has advantage on Dexterity ({@skill Stealth}) checks made to hide in snowy terrain.

### Actions
***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a {@dc 14} Strength saving throw or be knocked {@condition prone}.
***Cold Breath {@recharge 5}.*** The wolf exhales a blast of freezing wind in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a {@dc 12} Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 ({@damage 4d8}) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
