# Young White Dragon

*Large dragon, Chaotic Evil*

### Traits
***Ice Walk.*** The dragon can move across and climb icy surfaces without needing to make an ability check. Additionally, {@quickref difficult terrain||3} composed of ice or snow doesn't cost it extra movement.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.
***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. {@h}15 ({@damage 2d10 + 4}) piercing damage plus 4 ({@damage 1d8}) cold damage.
***Claw.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) slashing damage.
***Cold Breath {@recharge 5}.*** The dragon exhales an icy blast in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a {@dc 15} Constitution saving throw, taking 45 ({@damage 10d8}) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
