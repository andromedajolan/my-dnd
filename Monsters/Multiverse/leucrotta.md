# Leucrotta

*Large monstrosity, Chaotic Evil*

### Traits
***Mimicry.*** The leucrotta can mimic Beast sounds and Humanoid voices. A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations only with a successful {@dc 14} Wisdom ({@skill Insight}) check.
***Stench.*** Any creature other than a leucrotta or gnoll that starts its turn within 5 feet of the leucrotta must succeed on a {@dc 12} Constitution saving throw or be {@condition poisoned} until the start of the creature's next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the Stench of all leucrottas for 1 hour.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The leucrotta makes one Bite attack and one Hooves attack.
***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 1d8 + 4}) piercing damage. If the leucrotta scores a critical hit, it rolls the damage dice three times, instead of twice.
***Hooves.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) bludgeoning damage.

### Bonus Actions
***Kicking Retreat.*** Immediately after the leucrotta makes a Hooves attack, it takes the {@action Disengage} action.
