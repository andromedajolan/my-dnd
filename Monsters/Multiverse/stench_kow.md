# Stench Kow

*Large fiend (cattle), Unaligned*

### Traits
***Stench.*** Any creature other than a stench kow that starts its turn within 5 feet of the stench kow must succeed on a {@dc 12} Constitution saving throw or be {@condition poisoned} until the start of the creature's next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the Stench of all stench kows for 1 hour.
