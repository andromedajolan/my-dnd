### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The berbalang makes one Bite attack and one Claw attack.
***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 5} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 1d10 + 3}) piercing damage plus 4 ({@damage 1d8}) psychic damage.
***Claw.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 5} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 2d4 + 3}) slashing damage.

### Bonus Actions
***Spectral Duplicate (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).*** The berbalang creates one spectral duplicate of itself in an unoccupied space it can see within 60 feet of it. While the duplicate exists, the berbalang is {@condition unconscious}. A berbalang can have only one duplicate at a time. The duplicate disappears when it or the berbalang drops to 0 hit points or when the berbalang dismisses it (no action required). The duplicate has the same statistics and knowledge as the berbalang, and everything experienced by the duplicate is known by the berbalang. All damage dealt by the duplicate's attacks is psychic damage.
