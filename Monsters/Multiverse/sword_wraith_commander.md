# Sword Wraith Commander

*Medium undead, Lawful Evil*

### Traits
***Turning Defiance.*** The commander and any other sword wraiths within 30 feet of it have advantage on saving throws against effects that turn Undead.
***Unusual Nature.*** The commander doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The commander makes two Longsword or Longbow attacks.
***Longsword.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 1d8 + 4}) slashing damage, or 9 ({@damage 1d10 + 4}) slashing damage if used with two hands.
***Longbow.*** {@atk rw} {@hit 5} to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. {@h}6 ({@damage 1d8 + 2}) piercing damage.
***Call to Honor (1/Day).*** If the commander has taken damage during this combat, it gives itself advantage on attack rolls until the end of its next turn, and {@dice 1d4 + 1} {@creature sword wraith warrior|MPMM|sword wraith warriors} appear in unoccupied spaces within 30 feet of it. The warriors last until they drop to 0 hit points, and they take their turns immediately after the commander's turn on the same initiative count.

### Bonus Actions
***Martial Fury.*** The commander makes one Longsword or Longbow attack, which deals an extra 9 ({@damage 2d8}) necrotic damage on a hit, and attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn.
