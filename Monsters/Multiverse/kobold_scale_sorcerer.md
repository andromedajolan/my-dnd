# Kobold Scale Sorcerer

*Small humanoid, Any Alignment*

### Traits
***Pack Tactics.*** The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't {@condition incapacitated}.
***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom ({@skill Perception}) checks that rely on sight.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The kobold makes two Dagger or Chromatic Bolt attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Spellcasting.
***Dagger.*** {@atk mw,rw} {@hit 4} to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. {@h}4 ({@damage 1d4 + 2}) piercing damage.
***Chromatic Bolt.*** {@atk rs} {@hit 4} to hit, range 60 feet, one target. {@h}9 ({@damage 2d6 + 2}) of a type of the kobold's choice: acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder.
