# Nupperibo

*Medium fiend (devil), Lawful Evil*

### Traits
***Cloud of Vermin.*** Any creature, other than a devil, that starts its turn within 20 feet of one or more nupperibos must succeed on a {@dc 11} Constitution saving throw or take 5 ({@damage 2d4}) acid damage. ***Driven Tracker.*** In the Nine Hells, the nupperibo can flawlessly track any creature that has taken damage from any nupperibo's Cloud of Vermin within the previous 24 hours.
