# Prismari Professor of Perfection

*Small humanoid (sorcerer), Any Alignment*

### Traits
***Water Walking.*** The professor can walk across water and other liquids as if they were solid ground.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The professor makes three Tidal Strike attacks.

### Bonus Actions
***Rushing Wave.*** The professor is momentarily surrounded by a swirling wave of water and moves up to 30 feet. A creature can suffer this effect only once during a turn.
