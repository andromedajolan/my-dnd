# Fleecemane Lion

*Large monstrosity, Unaligned*

### Traits
***Keen Smell.*** The lion has advantage on Wisdom ({@skill Perception}) checks that rely on smell.
***Pounce.*** If the lion moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a {@dc 14} Strength saving throw or be knocked {@condition prone}. If the target is {@condition prone}, the lion can make one bite attack against it as a bonus action.
***Running Leap.*** With a 10-foot running start, the lion can long jump up to 25 feet.
***Spell Turning.*** The lion has advantage on saving throws against any spell that targets only the lion (not an area). If the lion's saving throw succeeds and the spell is of 4th level or lower, the spell has no effect on the lion and instead targets the caster.

### Actions
***Multiattack.*** The lion makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claw.
***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 1d8 + 4}) piercing damage.
***Claw.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}7 ({@damage 1d6 + 4}) slashing damage.
