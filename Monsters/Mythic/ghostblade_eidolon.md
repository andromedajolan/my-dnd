# Ghostblade Eidolon

*Medium undead, Any Alignment*

### Traits
***Blurred Form.*** Attack rolls against the eidolon are made with disadvantage unless the eidolon is {@condition incapacitated}.
***Incorporeal Movement.*** The eidolon can move through other creatures and objects as if they were {@quickref difficult terrain||3}. It takes 5 ({@damage 1d10}) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
***Turn Resistance.*** The eidolon has advantage on saving throws against any effect that turns undead.

***Ghostblade.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 6} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 ({@damage 1d8 + 3}) slashing damage plus 11 ({@damage 2d10}) force damage.
