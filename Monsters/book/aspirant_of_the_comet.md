# Aspirant of the Comet

*Medium humanoid, Any Alignment*

### Traits
***Hunger of the Void.*** When the aspirant is reduced to 0 hit points, its body and everything it is wearing or carrying, except for magic items, are sucked into a void and destroyed. Creatures in a 15-foot-radius sphere centered on the aspirant must make a {@dc 11} Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 10 ({@damage 3d6}) necrotic damage and is pulled 10 feet straight toward the aspirant's space. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only.
***Sinister Devotion.*** The aspirant has advantage on saving throws against the {@condition charmed} and {@condition frightened} conditions.
